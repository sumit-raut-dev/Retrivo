How it works

A simple 4-step process to report and recover lost items on campus.

Step 1

Report item

Fill out a form with details about the lost or found item, including location and description.

Step 2

Browse items

Search open reports by title, description, or location, and filter by category or type.

Step 3

Submit a claim

Share your contact details in the claim form, and get the reporter's contact back right away.

Step 4

Recover item

Meet up, confirm the item, and mark the report resolved from your dashboard.
